What is a Satellites job?

A satellite is like a robot that lives in space. Its job is to go round the Earth and take pictures or talk to other machines on the ground like your phone or TV. It is one of the best methods of talking to people over long distances and the only way, with out going to space yourself, that you can see what the earth looks like. Many people use satellites like scientists and engineers.
